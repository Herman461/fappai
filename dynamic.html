You may not use screen names, profile pictures or avatars that are offensive (e.g., names with explicit language), display symbols associated with gangs, hate groups, or terror organizations, or violate the content restrictions in Section 1.1 and 1.2 above.

Certain users may not use our services, regardless of their content. These are: gangs, hate groups, terror organizations, members of the foregoing, persons who are subject to U.S. sanctions and persons under the age of 18.

Our Terms of Service clearly and fully lay out rules that explicitly disallow our users from (among other things) harassing other users, interfering with our technical measures, spamming, or engaging in inauthentic behaviour. Spamming includes mass distribution of the same or similar content, content containing links intended to sell the user some dubious item, and the use of bots, scripts, or other automated tools for any purpose. Inauthentic behaviour includes creating fake accounts, liking or commenting on your own content using another account, and purchasing likes or comments from third-parties

If you see any content and/or user accounts that violate our terms of service and the guidelines set forth in articles 1.1, 1.2, 1.3, 2 and 3 then you may report individual GIFs by flagging it through the report function visible on all pieces of content.

To report a user account or for any additional issues, please email support@redgifs.com and clearly describe the issue and provide the appropriate links.

5. Enforcement

We endeavour to review all pieces of content and accounts flagged by our users and third parties.

5.1 Removals and Terminations

Violations may result in suspension or removal of videos, account privileges, or your entire account. Account removal will occur in severe cases, such as where you have wilfully or repeatedly violated our terms or have uploaded extremely inappropriate content. If your account is permanently removed, you may not create a new account.

In some cases, dependent on the reason for the content/profile removal, the appropriate authorities will be notified and all offending content shared with them.

If you believe your content and/or account has been removed without due reason then please email support@redgifs.com and make a request for the decision to be reconsidered.
